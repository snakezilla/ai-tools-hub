# AI Tools Hub Cold Email Campaign — Walkthrough

## Project Summary

Created a comprehensive Hormozi-style cold email campaign for AI Tools Hub targeting three high-ROI sectors. The campaign offers free resources and workshops as lead magnets to generate business.

---

## Research Conducted

### Repository & Website Review
- Analyzed [AI Tools Hub](https://ai-tools-hub-flax.vercel.app/) structure and offerings
- Reviewed `PROMPT.md` which references Hormozi's value equation framework
- Explored tools, workflows, and workshops pages

### Hormozi Framework Deep Dive
- **$100M Offers**: Value equation, no-brainer offers, risk reversal
- **$100M Leads**: Lead magnets, give-to-ask ratio, personalization at scale
- **Cold Outreach Strategy**: Value-first approach, 8+ touchpoints to convert

### Industry Research
- AI adoption rates across SMB sectors
- Pain points specific to marketing, accounting, and real estate
- ROI metrics (average $3.70 return per $1 invested in AI)

---

## Top 3 Sectors Selected

| Rank | Sector | Why | Key Pain Point |
|------|--------|-----|----------------|
| 1 | **Marketing Agencies** | 77% already experimenting with AI | Content creation bottlenecks |
| 2 | **Accounting Firms** | 85% enthusiastic, only 43% trained | 5-10 hrs/week on data entry |
| 3 | **Real Estate** | Surface-level adoption, huge upside | Lead follow-up delays |

---

## Deliverables Created

### 1. Cold Email Templates (9 total)
Three emails per sector following a proven sequence:
- **Email 1**: Value Bomb (free resource, no ask)
- **Email 2**: Curiosity/Pain Hook (ROI math)
- **Email 3**: Social Proof (testimonial + soft CTA)

### 2. Free Workshop Structure
- 60-minute hands-on format
- Guarantee: "Walk away with 1 working automation or I'll set it up free"
- No pitch, pure value delivery

### 3. Lead Magnet Strategy
- Sector-specific PDF checklists
- No signup required (builds trust)
- Naturally leads to workshop interest

### 4. Follow-Up Sequence
5 touches over 2 weeks across email and LinkedIn
