# AI Tools Hub Cold Email Campaign Strategy

A comprehensive Hormozi-style value-first cold outreach campaign to generate leads and fill workshops.

---

## Executive Summary

Based on deep research of the AI Tools Hub website, the Hormozi framework, and industry AI adoption trends, this plan outlines a cold email campaign offering **free resources and workshops** to businesses. The strategy follows Alex Hormozi's core principles:

- **Give to Ask Ratio**: Offer massive value upfront before asking for anything
- **Lead Magnets**: Free resources so good people would pay for them
- **No-Brainer Offers**: Make the free workshop too valuable to refuse
- **Personalization at Scale**: Industry-specific messaging that feels 1:1
